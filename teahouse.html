The Teahouse™ (and other written shenanigans)

Below you’ll find a collection of my stories, essays, and other assorted writing experiments. Some are more polished, some mildly unhinged, but all important to me in one way or another. For the sake of context, I’ve included bits of background here and there. This list is dynamic, meaning it may never really be fully complete (unless I stop being lazy and keep my leftovers somewhere…) and may continue to grow as long as I do. Funny thing.

Do note that the local versions of this story may be outdated in comparison to their live versions. They should be accurate as of ~April 2026.

Guanabara S/A (Guanabara Inc/O Cabresto)

Original Story | Political Drama

Technically, my very first original story! Over here, two brothers (Jorge and Denise) and a friend (Diana) end up joining a childhood friend of theirs (Cadu) in his political campaign for governor of the state of Rio de Janeiro. When Cadu wins, he ends up bringing over Jorge (and, unintentionally, Denise and Diana) to his administration in some way, and they have to deal with the consequences of their involvement in it. Does something here make sense? I hope so, but all I know is that what happens in Rio, stays in Rio.

I wrote this story with the placeholder title "O Cabresto", referring to voto de cabresto, a way of buying votes in the early 20th century. I used Guanabara S/A (Guanabara Inc.) in the end mostly because it was a more commercial-sounding name, even though I don’t use it often when referring to the story.

Só mais um Simpson (Just Another Simpson)

Adaptation, sorta

See analysis of the story here.

Okay, this one needs so many explanations that it warrants its own page! For starters: there is a The Simpsons fanfic titled No More Simpsons (Não Mais Simpsons), which happens in a world where Homer is a lazy drunk, Marge is significantly disturbed by her husband’s lack of care for her and the rest of the family, Bart is a sex-crazed maniac, Lisa has a best friend (!!!), and Maggie (herein Meg) is a boxer. It’s probably the longest Simpsons fanfic ever made in terms of chapters, standing at a whopping 218. Its author, a journalism student named Brendon, died in 2023, leaving the story without an ending. “Just Another Simpson” is essentially my version of the story’s three remaining chapters, intended as a homage to the Brendon.

Então Você Acredita Em Milagres... (So You Believe In Miracles...)

Story | TazerCraft | Fuga Impossível

This is actually a funny story that needs some additional info from me. In the past, I especially liked watching this Brazilian YouTube channel named TazerCraft, a duo who made several Minecraft series, including one that became known as "Fuga Impossível" (Impossible Escape"Fuga Impossível/Impossible Escape" was a 15-episode series that showed the lives of Pac (first on the left) and Mike (last on the right) in the infamous Alcatraz prison after a failed bank robbery. The reboot of a less-known 2012 series of same name, the new Impossible Escape became an instant hit at its release, becoming one of the channel’s most famous works.). This series, which was actually pretty influential for the year it was released (2015), got a revival when its backstory was essentially canonized on "QSMP" (the Quackity SMP), which at the time, still had a community.

Lacking familiarity with part of the QSMP cast team, I decided to do an Impossible Escape-themed oneshot. Due to some positive comments I received, a couple of months later I made it a full-fledged story, and people also liked it! Given how the SMP catastrophically imploded much later, I’m actually kinda surprised this story aged somewhat well. It’s in Portuguese, so I strongly encourage you to translate it using an external translator (such as DeepL) so you get less confused.
